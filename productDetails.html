Lunit INSIGHT CXR is an artificial intelligence solution designed to analyze chest X-ray images and detect various abnormalities. Through clinical research conducted with institutions such as Seoul National University Hospital, Massachusetts General Hospital of Harvard Medical School, UCSF, Grenoble Hospital in France, and the WHO-affiliated STOP TB Partnership, it has been demonstrated that the solution significantly aids radiologists in the timely early diagnosis of major lung diseases, including lung cancer, pneumonia, tuberculosis, and pneumothorax. Research also shows it greatly supports clinical physicians in environments without radiologists, with findings published in major clinical journals.

Overview

A medical imaging diagnostic support software designed to assist in image interpretation, utilizing Lunit’s proprietary deep learning-based AI algorithms to automatically analyze images, serving as a “Second Reader” to support physicians’ diagnoses. As a medical imaging diagnostic support software for chest X-ray interpretation, it detects suspected abnormalities related to major lung conditions (atelectasis, calcification, cardiomegaly, consolidation, pneumothorax, fibrosis, nodule/mass, pleural effusion, and pneumoperitoneum), aiding physicians in their diagnostic process.

Features and Performance

Approved by the Ministry of Food and Drug Safety (MFDS), certified with European CE medical device approval
Preparing for FDA approval (expected in 2021)

Offers services independent of equipment manufacturers

Delivers consistent results as a quantitative metric with high reproducibility and quantification through AI’s superior detection performance
